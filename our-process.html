Our Process

From First Call to Live Site

No guesswork, no surprises. Every Sixty Deuce project follows the same clear path, so you always know where things stand and what comes next.

Most web projects go sideways because there's no shared plan. Clients don't know what to expect. Designers disappear for weeks. Things get built that miss the mark, then get rebuilt. We designed our process specifically to prevent that. Every phase has a clear goal, a defined handoff, and a checkpoint where you approve before we move forward.

01

Phase 1

Discovery Call

schedule 30 minutes

Everything starts with a conversation. We want to understand your business, your goals, your customers, and what you need your website to do. This isn't a sales call. It's a planning session. We ask a lot of questions so we build the right thing, not just something that looks good.

What happens

  • check_circleWe learn your business, target audience, and goals
  • check_circleWe review any existing site or brand materials you have
  • check_circleWe discuss the scope: pages, features, timeline, and tier
  • check_circleYou ask us anything about how we work

You walk away with

  • arrow_forwardA clear project scope and recommended tier
  • arrow_forwardA realistic timeline and delivery estimate
  • arrow_forwardA fixed project quote. No hourly billing.
02

Phase 2

Kickoff and Content

schedule 1–2 days after agreement

Once the quote is agreed and a deposit is received, we send you a kickoff questionnaire. This is where you give us the raw material: your services, your story, your contact details, any photos or logos you have. You don't need to be a writer. We shape the copy. You just need to give us the facts.

What we need from you

  • check_circleServices you offer and service areas (if applicable)
  • check_circleBusiness name, contact info, and hours
  • check_circleLogo files (if you have them; we can work without)
  • check_circlePhotos of your work, team, or location (optional but helpful)
  • check_circleAny sites you like the look of for style reference

What we handle

  • arrow_forwardCopywriting and content structure
  • arrow_forwardSourcing and optimizing images if needed
  • arrow_forwardOrganizing all content into a page structure
03

Phase 3

Design and Build

schedule 3–15 business days depending on tier

This is where we do the work. We build the full site: layout, typography, color, responsive behavior, images, forms, SEO tags, and everything else. You won't see a wireframe phase or a separate mockup round. We design in the browser and build the real thing from the start, which means the first thing you see is close to final.

What we're building

  • check_circleResponsive HTML and CSS, fast, clean, no bloated frameworks
  • check_circleAll pages included in your tier
  • check_circleContact forms with spam protection
  • check_circleSEO tags: titles, descriptions, Open Graph, schema markup
  • check_circleOptimized images (WebP, lazy loading)
  • check_circleCross-device testing (phone, tablet, desktop)

Your role during this phase

  • arrow_forwardMinimal. We handle it.
  • arrow_forwardWe may follow up with questions on specific content
  • arrow_forwardYou'll receive a staging link when the build is ready for review
04

Phase 4

Review and Revisions

schedule 2–5 business days

You review the full site on a private staging link and send us feedback. We work through your notes and update the build accordingly. Each tier includes a set number of revision rounds, and we keep it organized so nothing gets missed or forgotten.

How revisions work

  • check_circleYou review the staging site on your own devices
  • check_circleSend consolidated feedback in one message per round
  • check_circleWe update and send a revised staging link
  • check_circleRepeat for your included number of revision rounds

Revision rounds by tier

  • T12 rounds
  • T23 rounds
  • T34 rounds
  • T4Scoped per project
05

Phase 5

Pre-Launch Checklist

schedule 1 business day

Before anything goes live, we run through a full pre-launch checklist. This isn't a quick scan. It's a systematic review of every technical detail that could affect your site's performance, discoverability, or user experience on launch day.

What we check

  • check_circleAll links tested: internal, external, and anchor links
  • check_circleContact form submissions verified end-to-end
  • check_circlePage titles, meta descriptions, and OG tags confirmed
  • check_circleImages optimized and rendering correctly on all screen sizes
  • check_circleSchema markup validated
  • check_circlerobots.txt and sitemap confirmed (T3 and T4)
  • check_circleGA4 and Search Console verified (T3 and T4)
  • check_circleSite speed reviewed and any obvious blockers resolved

What you receive

  • arrow_forwardFinal approval request before any domain changes
  • arrow_forwardNothing goes live without your sign-off
06

Phase 6

Launch

schedule Same day as approval

With your approval, we connect the domain, verify DNS propagation, and confirm the live site is rendering correctly. If you're migrating from an existing site, we coordinate the cutover to minimize downtime. The goal is a clean, zero-drama launch.

What we handle

  • check_circleDomain connection and DNS configuration
  • check_circleSSL certificate setup and HTTPS verification
  • check_circlePost-launch live site verification
  • check_circleRedirect setup if migrating from an old site
  • check_circleGoogle Search Console submission (T3 and T4)

You walk away with

  • arrow_forwardA live site, fully verified and working
  • arrow_forwardLogin credentials for any dashboards (GA4, Search Console, etc.)
  • arrow_forwardA summary of what was built and how to use it
